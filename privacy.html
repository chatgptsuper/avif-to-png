Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to AVIFtoPNG ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle your data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights.

The Data We Process

Our service is designed with privacy in mind. When you use our AVIF to PNG converter:

  • All image processing happens locally in your browser
  • Your images are never uploaded to our servers
  • We do not store or have access to your converted images
  • No user accounts or registration is required

Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage data to help us improve our service. This may include:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring website
  • Time and date of your visit
  • Pages viewed
  • Anonymous usage statistics (e.g., number of conversions)

This data is collected using cookies and similar technologies. It does not include any personally identifiable information.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to ensure the proper functioning of our website. We also use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our website. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For website usage analysis
  • CDN services: To deliver static content efficiently

These services may collect data as described in their respective privacy policies.

Data Security

We have implemented appropriate security measures to prevent your data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way. Since your images are processed locally in your browser, they are never exposed to external security risks on our end.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access
  • The right to rectification
  • The right to erasure
  • The right to restrict processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to object

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided below.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
